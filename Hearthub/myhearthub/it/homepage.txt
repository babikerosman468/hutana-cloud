# Guided Heart Coherence Practice

## 1. Breathe

Bring your attention to your heart. Take slow breaths — in for 5 seconds, out for 5 seconds.

## 2. Feel

Imagine breathing in and out through your heart. Bring up a feeling of appreciation — someone you love, a beautiful moment, gratitude.

## 3. Connect

Hold this feeling. Know your heart’s field expands and joins with the field of others practicing now.

Stay here 3–5 minutes. Return anytime. You are never alone in this field.
