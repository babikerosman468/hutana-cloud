## Guided Heart Coherence Practice

**1. Breathe:**  
Bring your attention to your heart. Take slow breaths — in for 5 seconds, out for 5 seconds.

**2. Feel:**  
Imagine breathing in and out through your heart. Bring up a feeling of appreciation — for someone you love, a moment of beauty, or gratitude.

**3. Connect:**  
Hold this feeling. Know your heart’s field expands as you feel it — and joins the field of others doing the same.

Stay here for 3–5 minutes. Return anytime. You are never alone in this field.
